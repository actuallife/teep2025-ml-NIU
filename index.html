2025 TEEP Machine Learning & Industry-Academia Collaboration Program

This international program offers hands-on learning and collaboration opportunities in machine learning and data analytics. Participants will gain practical skills, work with local students and companies, and explore the business applications of AI and data science in Taiwan.

Program Duration

July 1 to December 31, 2025 (minimum 5-month participation required)

Program Highlights

  • Academic courses: Fundamentals of Machine Learning, Data Analysis for Business Applications
  • Collaborative projects with local university students
  • Internships at local data technology companies
  • Final presentations and project reports

Scholarship

Participants will receive a monthly scholarship of NT$12,000 to support living expenses.

Eligibility

  • Bachelor’s degree holders or current master’s students
  • Background in machine learning, artificial intelligence, or data analytics

English Language Requirement

Only Vietnamese applicants must provide English proficiency certificates for visa purposes:

  • TOEFL PBT: 385 / TOEFL iBT: 26
  • IELTS: 3.0
  • TOEIC: 375

Fees

  • Administration Fee: NT$10,000 (cash payment)
  • Dormitory Fee: NT$9,500 for 2–4 months; additional NT$80/day for stays over 4 months

Accommodation

On-campus dormitory accommodation is available. Assistance will be provided to secure suitable housing.
