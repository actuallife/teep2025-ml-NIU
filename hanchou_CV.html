Profile

Dr. Han-Chou Lin is currently an Associate Professor in the Big Data Management and Application program at National Ilan University. His research focuses on predictive models, data mining, AI applications, and information management.

Education

  • Ph.D., Information Management, National Chengchi University
  • MBA, Business Management, National Ilan University
  • B.S., Information & Computer Education, National Taiwan Normal University
