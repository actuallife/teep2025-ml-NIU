Profile

Dr. Han-Chou Lin is an Assistant Professor at the Department of Big Data Management and Application, National Ilan University. He has extensive experience in teaching programming, artificial intelligence, data mining, and database systems. He also actively participates in academic-industry collaborative projects and talent cultivation initiatives.

Education

  • Ph.D., Information Management, National Chengchi University (2015)
  • MBA, Business Management, National Ilan University (2007)
  • B.S., Information & Computer Education, National Taiwan Normal University (2004)

TEEP 2025 Machine Learning & Industry-Academia Collaboration Program

Dr. Lin is actively involved in the TEEP 2025 (Taiwan Experience Education Program) hosted by National Ilan University, focusing on Machine Learning and Industry-Academia Collaboration.

The program offers international students hands-on experience with real-world problems, supervised projects with local businesses, and advanced training in AI, big data, and applied analytics. Dr. Lin serves as an academic mentor and project advisor for this initiative.

For more information, visit the official TEEP website.
